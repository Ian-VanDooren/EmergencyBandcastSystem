Are you a PNW musician in need of an Emergency Bandcast? Let us know.

MUSIC. LOVE. CULTURE. COMMUNITY.

Emergency Bandcast System is here to provide musicians with an online venue in which to perform, and to keep fans connected to and directly supporting the artists that they love. EBS was founded by Tacoma musician Jesse Turcotte in response to the widespread loss of gigs and closures of venues as a result of the 2020 pandemic.
